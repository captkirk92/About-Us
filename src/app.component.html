About StickerVerse

We're a passionate team of creators, designers, and print geeks dedicated to one thing: helping you make custom stickers that kick ass.

From a Garage to the Galaxy of Stickers

StickerVerse started in a small garage with a big dream: to make high-quality, custom stickers accessible to everyone. We were tired of complicated ordering processes, low-quality materials, and boring designs. So, we built the platform we wished existed.

Today, we've shipped millions of stickers to artists, brands, and makers around the world. But our core mission remains the same: to provide the best materials, the simplest process, and the most stellar customer service in the universe.
